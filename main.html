Maine Coon Cat

Described as the "Gentle Giant", Maine Coon is known with their large figure and thick fur coat. Despite being the largest house cat, Maine Coon is the most friendliest cat breed, makes them become the most popular cat breed!

Abyssinian Cat

Simply known as Abys, are the elegent and reagl-looking cats.Their distinctive orange agouti caot makes a resemblance to ancient Egyptian cats. They're especially love heights and immensely loyal to their owner!

Ragdoll Cat

Known with their crystal blue eyes, Ragdoll is named after their tendency to relax and go limp when picked up just like a rag doll. This breed is often referred as "puppy-cats" becuase of their dog-like personality, often seeking for human attention!

American Shorthair Cat

American shorthair is a medium-sized cat with thick and dense coat. They're really diverse in color, up to 60 different colors. This breed is known with keen intelligence, affectionate and low-maintenance. They can play well by themself and easily entertain themself for hours. This breed can live longer than 15 years!
